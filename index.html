How to add your SOCO photos

1. Save your photo files (JPG or PNG) in the same folder as this HTML file.
2. Open this file in a text editor (TextEdit on Mac, Notepad on Windows).
3. Find the SOCO section — search for Add photo.
4. Replace each placeholder block with: <img src="your-photo-name.jpg" alt="SOCO Travel">
5. Save and refresh in your browser to preview.

"I craft all my stories like a Shakespearean play — five acts, every time."

I tell stories and work behind the scenes to bring them to life. I lead creative people — videographers, editors, writers, talent — because I've been all of them. That fluency across the creative process is what makes the work land.

Over a decade spanning broadcast at Rogers Media and senior production roles at Metrolinx, I've built campaigns that move people — literally and figuratively. I can spot a flash frame at a mile. I understand how a transit corridor and a social feed share the same grammar: attention, movement, meaning.
